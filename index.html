Key Insights & Recommendations
⚠️
Margin squeeze is structural. Revenue grew 42% over 3 years, but net profit fell from $332K to $160K — OpEx grew 64%, nearly double the revenue growth rate.
💸
Labour is the #1 cost driver. All-in labour hit $1.34M in FY2025 — 33.6% of revenue, up from 31.1% in FY2023. Headcount stayed flat (34) but cost per head jumped 18% to $39.5K.
✅
Gross margin is improving. GPM rose from 59.3% → 63.0%, signalling better pricing power or COGS efficiency. The problem lies entirely in operating expenses.
🏭
Central kitchen is a long-term asset. $430K renovation + $231K equipment = $661K investment. Depreciation will peak in FY2026-27, then ease — watch for margin recovery.
💡
Recommended next step: Monthly cost-ratio monitoring via this dashboard. Target: labour below 30% of revenue and OpEx growth below revenue growth rate by FY2026.
